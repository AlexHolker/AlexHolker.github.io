What is Necromunda?

Necromunda is a tabletop skirmish game set in the Warhammer 40,000 universe, in which players command gangs fighting through the underbelly of the largest arcology on the world of Necromunda, Hive Primus.

The original boxed game was released in 1995, with an expansion Outlanders released the following year.

The Necromunda Living Rulebook is an update of the original game, released for free by Games Workshop's Specialist Games. It is no longer supported by Games Workshop, but survives in the form of Necromunda: Community Edition.

Necromunda: Underhive is the third official edition of the game, released in 2017. To learn more about Necromunda: Underhive, visit the official website.

About This Site

The house rules and utilities on this website are designed for use with the Necromunda Living Rulebook or Necromunda: Community Edition. If you've moved on to Necromunda Underhive you might be able to adapt this content for use in your campaign, but I can't make any promises.
