Vehicles

The following vehicles were created for use with the vehicle rulesNecromunda Magazine 4 written for the Ash Waste campaign setting.

Rhino

300+10D6 credits

©Mkhand Industries, 2017. Source

The Rhino has been the mainstay of the Imperial military since its inception. While no longer used by the Imperial Army, its reliability and ease of repair ensures that if one falls into civilian hands, it may be passed from user to user for hundreds of years.

Pintle Mount

The Rhino may be upgraded with a pintle mount for a basic or special weapon for 10 credits. It must be operated by a gunner.

Remote Weapon

The Rhino may be upgraded with a remote weapon mount for a basic or special weapon for 50 credits.

Fire Points

Up to two fighters may fire weapons from the top hatch.

Buttoned Down

At the start of your turn, if there are no enemies on board you may declare the Rhino Buttoned Down. If you do, the pintle mount weapon and top hatch may not be used and no crew may enter or leave the vehicle. The crew location is treated as AV 11 and boarders treat the vehicle as undefended.

Repressor

450+12D6 credits

©Mkhand Industries, 2017. Source

The Repressor is a dedicated crowd control vehicle, whose shielded fire ports and riot plow makes it a dangerous tool in the hands of any gang that manages to capture one.

Pintle Mount

The Repressor may be upgraded with a pintle mount for a basic or special weapon for 10 credits. It must be operated by a gunner.

Turret

The Repressor has a turret-mounted Storm Bolter. It must be operated by a turret gunner.

Fire Points

Up to six fighters may fire weapons from the fire ports in the rear compartment.

Buttoned Down

At the start of your turn, if there are no enemies on board you may declare the Repressor Buttoned Down. If you do, the pintle mount weapon may not be used and no crew may enter or leave the vehicle. The crew location is treated as AV 11 and boarders treat the vehicle as undefended.

Riot Plow

The Repressor is treated as Heavily Armoured from the front, adding an additional +1 AV, +1 ram damage and increasing the ram save to 5+.
